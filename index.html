My Personal Interests:

One of my favorite things to do, especially in the summer is go fly-fishing. This isn't just any type of fly-fishing, this is Japanese style fly-fishing called "Tenkara". It is a much more simplistic and easy to do. I would say if you are interested in fly-fishing at all, to try Tenkara first. It is very easy to pack and stow away, making it take less time to also get setup. My favorite river to fish in Utah currently is American Fork canyon. It is a tight spot, but all the more reason to be more stealth!

Another thing I really enjoy doing is taking my dogs to run in this field that not a lot of people know about. It is tucked away in a neighborhood, but completely open and very big. They could run and run all day long if I let them. One of my dogs is a golden retriever and she is a little more on the chunky side, she gets worn out the quickest. My other dog however loves to stalk the permimeter and make sure we are all safe. She is a Great Pyrenese and it definitely shows. I try to take them running at least 2-3 times in the week, but it makes it hard when it is so cold outside.

The last thing that I enjoy doing, and only in the summer time, is to go camping. Me and my wife bought a tent that actually fits to the bed of my truck, so it keeps us off of the ground and a lot warmer on the cold summer nights in the mountains. We were only able to go a few times last year, but this year I want to try and go almost every weekend! There are so many spots all over Utah to explore.
